How does fuel economy impact annual fuel cost?

Instructions

Hover A Dot to see the Cost and Recommended Vehicle. The Dot represents the average cost for all vehicles of that fuel economy score. The recommended vehicle is the lowest cost vehicle of that fuel economy score.

Retrospection

Some results that can be obtained from this chart is that annual costs trend linearly to fuel score and generally Forward Wheel Drive is significantly cheaper compared to other drive types.
