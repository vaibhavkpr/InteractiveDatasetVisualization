How different driving conditions can impact your fuel economy?

Instructions

The interactive bar plot allows us to investigate the relationship between various vehicle classes and their drive options to their fuel economy performance. You can select between the 6 vehicle classes and toggle between City / Highway fuel economy (measured in miles per gallon).

Retrospection

From the plot we can extract relationships such as smaller vehicles classes and 2-wheel / front wheel drive options in general result in better Fuel economy scores in both city and highway.
