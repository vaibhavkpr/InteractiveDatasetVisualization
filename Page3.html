How do different car engine specifications impact fuel economy?

Instructions

The interactive line plot allows us to investigate the relationship between various engine specifications and fuel economy metrics. You can select the fuel economy metric of interest as the y-axis, such as annual driving cost or fuel economy (measured in miles per gallon), and choose an engine specification to analyze by selecting the respective drop-down filters, such as engine cylinders, engine displacement, or fuel type.

Retrospection

From the plot we can extract relationships such as lower engine cylinders and displacements result in better Fuel economy and Save or Spend ratings.
